## Before We Dive In

### 1/12 Scale

Figma is not technically 1/12 scale. It's "kinda 1/12". That only means the sizes won't match perfectly between franchises since they prioritize proportions.

I personally divide them into these two categories:

- **Anime:** Similar proportions, but head/body dimensions vary by series. Parts are interchangeable, though some combinations look odd. Like a **Madoka Magica** head on a **Fate** figure.

- **Video Game:** Slim and long faces, more realistic than anime. **Persona**, **Fire Emblem**, **Zelda** figures fall here.

This matches the two types of Archetype bodies, so I'm not that crazy.

### Prices

Bodies can range from $20 to $100 USD (and even more), depending on the product line. Old discontinued products will be either too expensive or hard to find. If you find one at a good price, buy it.

I made a video about ["How to Buy Anime Figures](https://youtu.be/D4Pi0PO7iio?si=eXdshe5s50YVQhcV)" (in Spanish), one takeaway is you can use [BuyFinder](https://buyfinder.moe/) to search products by name, JAN, or MFC link ([example](https://myfigurecollection.net/item/118087)).

### Semi Seamless (AliExpress, Can't recall the brand)

These don't work with Figmas. They use ball-joint neck connections designed for doll heads, not Figma's peg system.

You'd need poster putty to make it work. Bad value too at $15, you're only saving $5 compared to a 1/6 scale body that's twice the size.

## Fixed Bodies

### S-Style

<!-- Image Source: https://miro.medium.com/1*VDCqrCRE5Zf6v-WOs0fS4w.png | Local: images/10/img-26.png -->
![](images/10/img-26.png)

This [line](https://myfigurecollection.net/entry/95891) is a variant from the [Figma x Fixed x Figure (FigFix)](https://myfigurecollection.net/entry/69140) line but produced by [FREEing](https://myfigurecollection.net/entry/7633) that also produces other lines like [B-Style](https://myfigurecollection.net/entry/99284) and [Y-Style](https://myfigurecollection.net/entry/99283). Same size as Figmas, but more akin to statues. That's where the "fix" comes from, lol.

And the **S** in **S-Style** stands for **swimsuits**!

The problem? Prices are inconsistent and can get ridiculous. Some characters cost $50, others $100+. If you want a specific character in a bikini and there's no regular Figma version (like from Fate or Figma Styles), this is your option.

**Examples**: [Akane](https://myfigurecollection.net/item/958722), [Hinatsuru Ai](https://myfigurecollection.net/item/806278), [QBZ-95](https://myfigurecollection.net/item/740095)
