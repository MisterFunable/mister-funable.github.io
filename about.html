About My Doll Inventory

Purpose

This platform brings together scattered information about one-sixth scale dolls and more. It’s my way of keeping track of what I collect, documenting details for my videos, and sharing the tips and knowledge I’ve picked up over the years, while continuing to discover new ones along the way.

Site Featuers

  • Product Identification and Search

    Not every site keeps historical data, so yeah, if no one will do it, I'll do it myself.

  • Knowledge Sharing

    Talking with people is weird, so there are guides. Otherwise you can reach out via YouTube, Instagram or Discord. Eventually I'll see it.

  • Image Archive

    We need to protect uncensored images - it's our responsibility for next generations that will regret not seeing products that won't be released anymore.
